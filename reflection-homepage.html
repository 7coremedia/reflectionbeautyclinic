Our Approach

We believe luxury
skincare shouldn't
be a mystery.

Every Reflection product is built on peer-reviewed science, honest ingredient lists, and a deep respect for your skin's natural intelligence. Our clinic team works alongside our formulators — testing on real clients, observing real results.

Formulated for African skin
Our products are developed and tested in Lagos, on the skin types that actually walk through our clinic doors — not adapted from European formulations.
No proprietary blends. Full transparency.
We list every active, every concentration. You know exactly what you're putting on your skin and why it's there.
Clinic-to-consumer pipeline
Products are only released once they've been validated in our treatment rooms. If it doesn't work in the clinic, it doesn't reach the shelf.
Sustainable & cruelty-free
No animal testing at any stage of development. We're working towards refillable packaging and reduced-footprint production.
